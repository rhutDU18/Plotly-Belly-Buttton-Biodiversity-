Roza is a biological researcher who works with discovering new bacterial species which can synthesize proteins that taste like beef. Her lab has partnered with Improbable Beef to search for bacteria that will provide the closest resemblance in terms of taste. The human body is a large source for harboring species particularly in the gut. The variation between individuals is center to Rozas hypothesis that one of the participants will contain the right type of bacteria in their navels which would be a breakthrough for Rozas lab and Improbable Beef.

We helped Rosa build a drop-down array containing her sample demographic which lets individual participants select their ID numbers. We also created a dashboard with a bar, bubble, and gauge chart to let them see which types of species are contained within their navels.
